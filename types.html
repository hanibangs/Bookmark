TYPES OF BOOKMARK

Traditional Bookmarks:

These are rectangular or strip-shaped bookmarks made from paper, cardstock, or plastic. They often feature decorative designs, quotes, or illustrations.

Metal Bookmarks:

Metal bookmarks are durable and long-lasting. They come in various shapes and designs, often featuring intricate patterns or embellishments.

Magnetic Bookmarks:

Magnetic bookmarks have two magnetic pieces that clip onto the page, holding the bookmark in place. They're convenient and easy to use, as they won't slip out of the book.

Fabric Bookmarks:

Fabric bookmarks are made from cloth materials like cotton, silk, or felt. They can be plain or adorned with embroidery, beads, or other decorations.

Leather Bookmarks:

Leather bookmarks are stylish and elegant, often featuring embossed designs or stitching. They're durable and can last for a long time with proper care.

Corner Bookmarks:

Corner bookmarks are designed to clip onto the corner of a page, holding it in place. They're compact and won't protrude from the book like traditional bookmarks.

Tassel Bookmarks:

Tassel bookmarks feature a tassel attached to the top or bottom of the bookmark. They add a decorative touch and are often used in journals or planners.

Ribbon Bookmarks:

Ribbon bookmarks are made from lengths of ribbon attached to a decorative charm or bead. They're versatile and can be easily customized to match the book or reader's preferences.

Elastic Band Bookmarks:

Elastic band bookmarks have a stretchy band that wraps around the front cover of the book, holding the bookmark in place. They're ideal for keeping books securely closed during travel.

Personalized Bookmarks:

Personalized bookmarks can be customized with names, initials, or photos, making them unique and special to the owner.
